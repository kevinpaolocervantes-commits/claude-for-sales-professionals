# Account Research Agent

## Objective

Use Claude Code to generate a complete account briefing package.

## Inputs

- Company Name
- Website
- Industry
- Product Being Sold

## Outputs

- Executive Summary
- Company Overview
- Recent News
- Growth Signals
- Potential Challenges
- Suggested Stakeholders
- Discovery Questions
- Recommended Outreach Angles

## Example Prompt

Research the following company and generate an executive-level account briefing.

Company:
[Company Name]

Website:
[Website]

Industry:
[Industry]

Product Being Sold:
[Your Product]

Deliver:

1. Executive Summary
2. Company Overview
3. Recent Business Signals
4. Potential Business Challenges
5. Relevant Stakeholders
6. Discovery Questions
7. Personalized Outreach Angles
