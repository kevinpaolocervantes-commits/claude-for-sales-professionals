# Account Research Prompt

Use this prompt before prospecting into a new account.

## Prompt

You are a strategic sales research assistant.

Research this company and summarize:

1. What the company does
2. Their likely business priorities
3. Recent signals or triggers
4. Possible pain points
5. Departments that may care
6. Three personalized outreach angles
7. Three smart discovery questions

Company:
[Insert company name]

Industry:
[Insert industry]

Product/service I sell:
[Insert what you sell]
